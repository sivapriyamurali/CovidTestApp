Who qualifies for Testing?

Testing is available for ages 3 and up. Minors under 16 must be accompanied by an adult.

How does it work?

  1. Register as a new patient. Select a test site and available time by logging in.
  2. Insurance card and proof of identity are required Be sure to have your mobile phone in case we need to reach you.
  3. Upon arrival, follow instructions posted at the site.
    • For rapid result tests, our provider will call with next steps, and patients may be asked to come into the clinic depending on their results.
    • For lab tests, your sample is sent to one of our lab partners. Average turnaround time for receiving results from lab tests is 1-2 days.
  4. We'll text you a secure link to view and download your results for 5 days.

    We'll also send you emails to activate a your account, which will give you permanent access to your results.
